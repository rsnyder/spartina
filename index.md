## Welcome to Spartina.io!

Spartina is a small digital workbench for exploring artificial intelligence, emerging technologies, and practical ways to use them. The blog includes hands-on experiments, project notes, and straightforward informational posts about tools, techniques, and ideas worth understanding. The emphasis is on practical exploration: learning what these technologies can, and can’t, do in real situations.

I also offer a few practical technical services for residents of Sun City Hilton Head, including Mac help, simple websites, digital archiving, photo scanning, and AI tutoring. Details are available on the [Services](/services)￼page.
